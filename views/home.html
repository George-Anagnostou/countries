Welcome to the country guessing site. There are currently only a few things to do, but hopefully you will find them interesting. Namely, the Continents section is a good spot to look through all of the countries in the world, filterable by continent, and the Country Guesser page is a simple guessing game based on country flags and population. In the future, more games will be added, such as a population guessing version, and hopefully a few more.

Learning is largely through repetition, and it should be fun. I found that playing games and creating recognizable patterns and associations make learning not only easier, but more effective and enjoyable. I hope that playing the games on this site is not only fun, but productive towards learning about the countries of the world.

This site is a little unique: it uses no JavaScript. Everything is written in plain HTML and CSS, and it's all delivered and handled by a simple Go server.

The guessing games on this site use cookies to compare your guess to the question asked, as well as handle user sessions.
